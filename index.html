Short Bio

 I am a third year CS Ph.D. student at Brown University working with Prof. Chen Sun and a student researcher at Google Deepmind. Previously, I obtained my bachelor degree in software engineering at Tsinghua University in 2021, and worked as a student researcher in Google Research in 2022.

My research interest is multimodal learning and video understanding. In detail, I'm especially interested in squeezing knowledge from large-scale foundation models for downstream multimodal tasks and structural concept learning for vision-language models. Feel free to contact me for collaborations and casual chats. I'm looking for research-related internship opportunities in 2024 summer, looking forward to any potential opportunities!
